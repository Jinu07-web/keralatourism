About kerala

Kerala, a state in the southwestern region of India, is known for its unique geographical features and rich cultural heritage. It is bordered by Karnataka to the north and northeast, Tamil Nadu to the east and south, and the Arabian Sea to the west. Kerala's landscape is characterized by its lush greenery, extensive backwaters, hill stations, and beautiful beaches.
